Sacred Geometry

Mandalas represent the cosmos metaphysically and symbolically. Each pattern is unique, generated through mathematical algorithms that mirror nature's own creative processes.

Fractal Dimensions

Self-similar patterns that reveal infinite complexity. These mathematical forms appear throughout nature, from coastlines to clouds, representing the underlying order of chaos.

Algorithmic Expression

Each artwork is generated in real-time using WebAssembly, combining computational efficiency with artistic expression to create unique, never-repeating patterns.

Nise da Silveira

This work honors Nise da Silveira (1905-1999), a Brazilian psychiatrist who revolutionized mental health treatment through art and compassion. She believed in the healing power of creative expression and saw art as a window into the unconscious mind. Her work at the Museum of Images of the Unconscious continues to inspire artists and healers worldwide.
